Wings Brigade

Wings Brigade General Application

The 101st Combat Aviation Brigade — the Wings Brigade — is the division's aviation unit, tasked with executing air assault operations and providing aerial support for ground forces. Pilots graduating from the brigade's education programs in Kronos and Blizzard can specialize further across three companies: Air Logistics, Air Assault, and Pilot Qualification. A more comprehensive overview of the brigade and its command structure is on the opening page of the application.

Wings Application Application Tracker

Benefits

  • Fly for unique air assault and aerial combat operations across a variety of helicopters, on- and off-base
  • Enroll in or teach challenging courses to expand your career
  • Air Assault Badge and Aviator Badge earned by being here and completing your education
  • Officers stay commissioned; enlisted personnel earn quick promotions for OCS
  • Badge and medal opportunities through events and external courses

  • [4x] Senior Logistical Pilot (O2, 1LT – ALC)
  • [2x] Special Tactical Warfare Pilot (O2, 1LT – AAC)
  • [∞] Logistical Pilot (O1, 2LT – ALC)

Experience isn't required to join ALC or PQC at any position. AAC requires Blizzard experience.

Aurora Company

One of the two main companies within the Black Hats Brigade, considered the foundation of the instructing unit. Aurora Company hosts phases for Air Assault School, teaching new personnel rappelling, airborne operations, and inspections. Known for maturity and proficiency.

"Do It Right."

Positions Available

1x Assistant Senior Black Hat (O1) Black Hat (E6/E7)

Requirements

  • E2+ to apply for Black Hat, E7+ for ASBH
  • Account age 90+ days
  • Able to meet quota and willing to pass BHT
  • Active, dedicated, mature, and professional
  • No recent arrests or dishonourable discharges
  • Able to communicate and cooperate with other staff
  • Instructor experience (optional)

Benefits

  • Fun and active community
  • Promotion from E6 to E7 through activity (BH)
  • Promotion to SBH or CHICOM (ASBH/SBH)
  • Access to a plethora of USAR courses
  • Regular competitions with prizes
  • Held to a higher standard than the rest
  • Air Assault Badge after 2 weeks of service

Titan Company

One of two companies in the Black Hats Brigade. Titan hosts the Air Assault Course, allowing members of other divisions and commands to earn the Air Assault Badge, while also running the Air Assault School that teaches incoming personnel the basics of the division.
